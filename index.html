About

  • I am a PhD candidate in Mathematics at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
  • I am currently a visiting research student at UNSW Sydney.
  • My research focus is analytic number theory, encompassing the estimation of exponential sums, the circle method, the sieve method, and other related techniques.

Research Interests

Interests

  • Diophantine equation
  • Exponential sums
  • Character Sum Estimation
  • Other fields related to number theory, such as harmonic analysis and dynamical systems

Current directions

  • Sums of Kloosterman and Gauss Sums
  • Large sieve inequality over short intervals
