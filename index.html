About

  • I am a PhD candidate in Mathematics at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, expecting to complete my PhD in March 2027.
  • I am currently a visiting research student at UNSW Sydney, working under the supervision of Prof. Igor E. Shparlinski.
  • My primary research area is analytic number theory, especially exponential sums, character sums, bilinear forms, and arithmetic distribution problems.

Research Interests

Areas of interest

  • Analytic number theory
  • Exponential sums, character sums, and bilinear forms
  • Kloosterman sums, Gauss sums, and Salié sums
  • Large sieve inequalities and arithmetic distribution problems
  • Harmonic analysis and additive combinatorics in number theory

Current directions

  • Fractional parts of sums of square roots
  • Bilinear sums involving Kloosterman and Gauss sums
  • Shifted bilinear sums of Salié sums and the distribution of modular square roots of shifted primes
  • Large sieve inequalities for character sums over short intervals
  • Square-free parts of palindromes and related applications of sieve methods
