About

  • I am a PhD candidate in Mathematics at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
  • I am currently a visiting research student at UNSW Sydney, working under the supervision of Prof. Igor E. Shparlinski.
  • My primary research area is analytic number theory, especially exponential and character sums and their applications in number theory.

Research Interests

Interests

  • Distribution of rational points on curves and more general varieties over finite fields
  • Exponential and character sums and their applications in number theory
  • Harmonic analysis and additive combinatorics in number theory

Current directions

  • Shifted bilinear sums of Salié sums and the distribution of modular square roots of shifted primes
  • Kloosterman and Gauss sums
  • Large sieve inequalities over short intervals
  • Estimating exponential sums related to the Möbius function via the Delta circle method
  • Fractional parts of sums of square roots
  • The square-free part of the palindromes
